Frequently Asked Questions

Which security camera has the best battery life?
Eufy cameras offer the longest battery life at up to 365 days with HomeBase and solar panel integration, compared to Arlo's 3-6 months and Ring's 6-12 months. This is due to Eufy's power optimization and local processing capabilities.
Is Eufy better than Ring for privacy?
Yes, Eufy offers superior privacy with local storage options and no cloud dependency, while Ring stores all data in Amazon's cloud and has established partnerships with law enforcement agencies that can access user footage.
Which security camera has no monthly fees?
Eufy offers the most features without monthly fees through local storage and HomeBase processing. Both Arlo and Ring require subscriptions ($3-20/month) for advanced features like cloud storage, AI detection, and extended recording.
What's the best security camera for Amazon Alexa users?
Ring provides the best Alexa integration since it's owned by Amazon, with native Echo Show support and voice control. However, Eufy and Arlo also work with Alexa, just with fewer native features.
Which camera has the best video quality?
Arlo offers the highest video quality with 4K HDR recording and color night vision technology. Eufy provides good 2K-4K options, while Ring typically maxes out at 1536p resolution.
Can I use these cameras without internet?
Eufy works best offline with local storage through HomeBase. Ring and Arlo require internet connectivity for most features, though Ring offers limited local storage on some models.
Which brand offers the best customer support?
Ring generally receives the highest customer support ratings due to Amazon's infrastructure, followed by Arlo. Eufy's support is adequate but can be slower for complex technical issues.
